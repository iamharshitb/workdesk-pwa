Navigating the App
4 tabs · what each one does
▼
⊞
Dashboard
Your home screen. Shows your personal greeting, streak, focus timer, task stats, progress bar, and your tasks split across three tabs — My Tasks, By Member, and Follow-up.
＋
Input
Register new tasks for the team, assign members, set priority, start date, and due date. Also shows all active tasks with a date range filter for historical lookup.
▤
Report
Monthly A4 report (MBC tasks only), weekly digest with task and comms summary, your personal My Report, and the Comms Dashboard from your Google Sheet.
📅
Calendar
Communications planning calendar. Add planned communications to any date with subject, type, and people involved. Track status as Planned → Sent → Delayed.
⊞
Dashboard Features
Greeting · Timer · Tasks · Streak
▼
👋
Greeting Bar
Shows a personalised greeting with your name, how many tasks are in progress, and any critical tasks. Updates based on time of day.
🔥
Streak Counter
Tracks consecutive days where at least one task was completed. Weekends are forgiven automatically — only weekday gaps break the streak.
⏱
Focus Timer
A Pomodoro-style countdown timer. Choose 30m, 45m, or a custom duration. Uses wall-clock time so it stays accurate even when your phone is locked.
📊
Stats & Progress
Four pills — Total, In Prog, Done, Critical. Tap any pill to filter My Tasks to that category. The progress bar only counts MBC tasks, not individual ones.
📋
My Tasks Tab
Your assigned tasks in compact rows. Tap any row to expand — see member details, action buttons, a 🔔 reminder button, and a comment thread. Done tasks collapse into a monthly archive at the bottom.
👥
By Member Tab
Every team member shown as a collapsible card, sorted by completion % (leaderboard). Tap a card to see their active tasks and done history. Harshit (admin) can mark anyone's tasks done.
🔗
Follow-up Tab
Shows tasks assigned to teammates that are still in progress — so you can follow up with them without having to ask around.
💡
Pull down on the dashboard to refresh data. Calendar communications due within 7 days also appear at the top of My Tasks as purple comms cards.
＋
Registering Tasks
MBC vs Individual · Priority · Due dates
▼
🏢
MBC Task
Team task that contributes to department output. Shows in monthly reports, progress bar, and team stats. Default type when registering a task.
👤
Individual Task
Personal tasks like mandatory training, laptop upgrades, etc. Only visible to you and the admin. Does not affect team stats or appear in management reports. Still counts toward your streak.
🔔
Due Date
Optional deadline. Tasks due today are pinned to the top of My Tasks with a red highlight and pulsing 🔴 badge. Tasks due in 1–3 days show an amber ⏰ warning.
⚡
Priority Levels
Critical (red), High (amber), Medium (teal), Low (grey). Critical tasks show in the stats pill and are never hidden from view.
💡
Each member gets their own "Respective Task" field — what their specific contribution to the overall task is. This shows in the expanded card and in their personal report.
📅
Communications Calendar
Plan · Track · Report
▼
➕
Adding a Communication
Tap any date on the calendar → a bottom sheet opens. Enter subject, pick the type (Video, Blog, Email, Event, Social, Livestream, Other), and list people involved.
🔵
Status Tracking
Each communication has a status — Planned 🔵, Sent ✅, or Delayed ⚠️. Tap the status badge in the modal to cycle through. Sent items disappear from My Tasks automatically.
📋
Year History
A collapsible section at the bottom of the calendar page shows all communications for the current year, grouped by month with status indicators.
📊
Appears in Reports
Calendar communications appear in the Weekly Digest (this week's sent/planned/delayed) and the Monthly A4 Report (all sent communications for the month).
💡
The calendar is shared — all team members see the same data in real time. If you're listed as a person involved, the communication appears in your My Tasks within 7 days of its date.
▤
Reports
Monthly · Weekly · My Report
▼
📄
Monthly A4 Report
Two-page formatted report for leadership. Page 1: MBC task summary with ring chart, member contributions, and communications sent. Page 2: Comms Dashboard from your Google Sheet CSV. Export as PDF with one tap.
📊
Weekly Digest
Tap 📊 Weekly to generate a summary of this week — tasks done, in progress, team contributions, and communications sent. Copy with one tap for WhatsApp.
👤
My Report
Personal monthly report showing all tasks (MBC + Individual) for any team member. Admin (Harshit) can switch between members using the selector. Non-admins see only their own report.
💡
Individual tasks never appear in the main A4 report — only in My Report. This keeps the management report focused on team output.
💡
Tips & Tricks
Quick notes · Reminders · Themes
▼
📝
Quick Notes
A personal scratchpad (500 char limit) at the bottom of the Dashboard. Syncs across all your devices in real time — write on your PC, read on your phone.
🔔
Task Reminders
Expand any task in My Tasks and tap 🔔 Remind me. Set a date and time — when it fires you'll get a sound and a toast. Tap Snooze to reschedule, Dismiss to remove.
🎨
Themes
6 themes available — Standard, Frosted Glass, iOS Fans, Dark, Light, and Neon. Tap 🎨 Theme in the top bar to switch. Your choice is saved per device.
📲
Install as App
On Android: open the site in Chrome, tap ⋮ menu → Add to Home Screen. The app will install with the Workdesk icon and open fullscreen like a native app.
🔄
Pull to Refresh
On the Dashboard, swipe down from the top of the page to manually refresh members and tasks. The real-time listener keeps data live automatically.
